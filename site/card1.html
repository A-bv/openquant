the principle

Money later is worth less than money now.

A deal pays you in the future. Start simple: what is a single $100 promised later worth to you today?

the test

Is the stream worth its upfront cost?

The deal costs you money today. The net value is what the stream is worth today, minus that cost. Positive means it adds value.
